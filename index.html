FAQ

1 What is the process to place the order?

Please fill the enquiry form. We will contact you to analyze your needs. Once the design gets finalized you can expect the delivery within 45 to 60 days. You can also meet our experienced designers in person & avail free design consultation at our Experience Centres.

2 How much money should I pay in advance to start the work?

For customised products we expect 50% of the Final Quotation to be paid in advance during design sign-off. Once the material is manufactured and ready to shipping you will need to pay the rest of the amount.

3Does the final cost price include GST and other taxes?

Yes, the final cost price includes GST and all other taxes. There are no extra charges.

4 How much do you charge for shipping of the product?

No, you don’t have to pay anything for the shipping.

5 Will there be any extra delivery charges if my apartment is on the 2nd floor and above?

We don’t charge any extra charges if your apartment has a lift. But for apartments without lifts a minimal fee of 3000 will be charged if we have to deliver heavy products to the house located in 2nd floor or above.

6 Can I return the product if I don’t like it? What is the return policy?

No, we make great efforts to satisfy each and every customer with our design and quality. And we haven’t received any request for return till date. So we are sure that you will love our masterpieces for sure.

7 Can I track my order?

We have a dedicated Customer Relationship Manager for all our Customers who will be in touch with our customers for stepwise updates.

MAGNON INTERIORS

Interior design plays a vital role in transforming the functionality, aesthetics, and overall appeal of any property, be it a residence, office, retail space, or other building types. Here’s why interior design holds such significance for all kinds of properties, especially when you partner with an interior designer in Bangalore.

Aesthetic Appeal: Interior designers wield the power to craft visually captivating and harmonious spaces through the artful selection of color schemes, furniture, decor, and lighting. A well-designed interior can instantly make a space more inviting, creating an atmosphere that enhances the overall appeal.


Functionality: Interior designers are experts in optimizing space to ensure it serves its intended purpose efficiently. They consider the flow of movement within a space, the strategic placement of furniture, and the organization of elements to enhance functionality and user-friendliness.

Comfort and Well-being: Interior design significantly impacts the comfort and well-being of occupants. Elements like ergonomic furniture, appropriate lighting, and effective temperature control contribute to creating a healthier and more comfortable living or working environment.

Personalization: Interior design enables property owners to express their unique style and preferences. Whether it’s a cozy, rustic home or a sleek, modern office, interior designers work diligently to reflect the client’s individual tastes and needs.


Space Optimization: Interior designers excel at making the most of available space. They can devise creative solutions to maximize storage, reduce clutter, and create the illusion of more space, a particularly valuable skill in smaller properties.

Increased Property Value: A well-designed interior can significantly augment a property’s value. Prospective buyers or renters are often willing to pay a premium for an attractive and functional space.

Branding and Image: In the realm of commercial properties, interior design serves as a potent tool for branding and cultivating a distinctive corporate image. The design can effectively communicate a company’s values and culture, leaving a lasting imprint on clients, employees, and visitors.

Energy Efficiency: Interior design can incorporate energy-efficient elements such as sustainable materials, efficient lighting, and proper insulation. This approach can lead to long-term cost savings and a reduced environmental footprint.


Psychological Impact: Colors, textures, and layout have a profound psychological impact on individuals. Interior designers consider these factors to create spaces that promote relaxation, productivity, or other desired emotional states.

Compliance and Safety: Interior designers possess in-depth knowledge of building codes and safety regulations. They ensure that the design meets all necessary requirements, thus averting potential legal issues.

Long-Term Satisfaction: A well-conceived interior design stands the test of time, offering enduring satisfaction. It reduces the need for frequent renovations or updates, resulting in significant time and cost savings.
