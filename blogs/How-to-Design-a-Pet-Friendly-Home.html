How to Design a Pet Friendly Home

Pets remain one of the most pampered and decent creatures on this earth, they need to be kept in a comfortable space, adjustable weather, and physical conditions. A pet’s home has unique decor that needs to be maintained, that is pet friendly. These furnishings are available in a variety of Colours, patterns, textures, and a lot more. It’s a little bundle of joy, which makes your life happy and interesting. The small 4 legs, speechless family members need to be kept in a comfortable space, without even destroying the look of your apartment. Some People recommend laminate, marble, stone, vinyl for flooring in various bedrooms. All the bedrooms must be kept clean, preventing animals from getting certain infections. The surface should be smooth so that the hands and nails of animals do not get stuck. A pet must have defined space, just like how we have. The room must include different bed furniture, just like a bed, some interesting toys, an eating table, and a lot more. All of this must be appealing and must also look useful. It is necessary to have an air purifier at your home when you have plenty of animals. Having an air purifier helps you from getting rid of minor allergic hair particles. Not only this, it prevents you from inhaling pet hairs. This is advised to be one of the most important products that need to be in an animal house. Apart from this, you need to keep your house clean, by dusting and vacuuming your house daily. Keep your pets well-groomed, give them regular vaccines. Make sure that they take bath regularly and do not stink. Adjust a perfect color scheme to your pet furniture, make it as colorful as possible, or combine it with your interior design.

Go for fabrics and flooring materials that are easy to manage. It is possible to wipe clean leather or ultra-suede fabric and will not be drastically affected by regular wear. A synthetic germ- and stain-resistant alternative made with pet owners in mind is Crypton Super Fabric. .It is available in several custom colors and designs, and couture pet beds, furniture covers, etc.



Look for the hazards of choking, strangulation, electrocution, and suffocation. Keep cords for window treatment short and cut through any loops, and unplug wires and electrical cords or cover them. Eliminate “ladders” that can be climbed by curious pets to reach elevated areas such as countertops and tabletops.

Make sure that indoor plants are pet-safe styles. Lilies can cause cats to suffer from kidney failure. Amaryllis, poinsettia, mums, and aloe vera are other common, but toxic, plants. Animals can often maneuver open cupboards to access products for home cleaning, pesticides, fertilizers, and other hazardous items. Just think about latching them shut. Keep rooms where rodenticides and traps are placed off-limits.

If you have a dog at home make sure that you also have a dog bed colorful and stylish with various soft toys. Nowadays you get trendy beds, in different shapes and sizes. Then also fit in some amazing wallpapers, with different patterns with some animal theme. Make sure that you design a room for your pet, which is just like a room of yours. All you need to make sure is that your pet feels comfortable and cozy in each and everything that you use, starting from materials to the blanket you use. Apart from this if you have a cat as a pet, do not keep them in cages let them free. Did you know that cats love to hide, climb spaces and make sure that you have healthy spaces at your home where your cat can play and enjoy? Make these play area small sleek and elegant. A cat also requires a bed, have a small cozy bed just like a baby’s cradle.

There are certainly other elements that you need to keep in mind. First of all, do not buy expensive rugs. Find out durable, cheap, and stylish rugs for your home. Not only this, it is not preferred to have a wall-to-wall carpet, this is because carpets absorb odor and smell. It is difficult to clean carpets especially when you have pets at home. You can prefer having painted concrete floors which are lovely and durable. All you need to do is just simply mop the floor. Though there are always two sides to a coin, pets can scratch the floor.
