About Magnon

Know Us

At Magnon, we're committed to crafting interior designs that transcend trends and stand the test of time. We believe in creating spaces that are not just relevant today, but will remain equally inspiring and functional even in the years to come. We understand that your home is a long-term investment, and we take pride in designing spaces that you will cherish for a lifetime.



MAGNON: Your Ultimate Interior Design Partner :
At Magnon, we offer comprehensive interior design services tailored to meet your every need. From concept to execution, our team ensures a seamless and hassle-free experience, bringing your vision to life with precision and style. Here's an overview of our structured approach:
Initial Consultation:

  • We start by understanding your requirements, preferences, and budget.
  • Our team carefully listens to your vision and needs to create a personalized plan.
Conceptualization and Design:
  • Based on your input, we develop a design concept that reflects your style.
  • We use mood boards, sketches, and renderings to visualize the design for you.



Material Selection:

  • Our experts help you select materials, finishes, and fixtures that align with your aesthetic and budget.
  • We consider factors like durability and functionality to ensure your satisfaction.
Budgeting and Cost Estimation:
  • We provide a detailed cost estimate to keep the project within your budget.
  • Our team works closely with you to manage expenses and avoid surprises.
Project Planning and Scheduling:
  • A detailed project plan with milestones and deadlines is established.
  • We manage the schedule to ensure timely completion of your project.



Execution and Construction:

  • We take charge of the entire construction process, from hiring contractors to quality control.
  • Our team ensures that every aspect of the design is executed flawlessly.
Procurement and Installation:
  • We procure all necessary materials, furniture, and fixtures on your behalf
  • Our team coordinates delivery and installation to bring your design to life.

Experience the MAGNON difference and transform your space into a masterpiece. Contact us today to schedule your consultation and take the first step towards your dream interior.
